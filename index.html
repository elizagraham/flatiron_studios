FIT OR FLOP®

The online reality show, Fit or Flop, needed a short promo to be run as a stand-alone piece used to promote the upcoming season, and as the opening to each of its episodes. The green screen and off-site production and shoot required the hiring of a voice-over actor, motion graphics, titling and animation. The spot showcased the competition's energetic and interesting contestants, while building anticipation for the show's release.

Play Demo

USA SEVENS RUGBY®

The USA Sevens Rugby tournament needed an engaging commercial to highlight its premier event in Las Vegas. The spot which was ran in several markets on NBC showcased the event's 16 elite sevens rugby teams competing in the HSBC Sevens World Series and its super passionate fans from across the globe in celebrating the newest Olympic Sport. The large production required permits and permissions for shooting in casinos and on the Strip, transportation for hired cast and crew throughout the city, and the handling/rentals of high-quality production equipment.
